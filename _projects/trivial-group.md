# Trivial Group

Trivial Group is a boutique AI development and consulting company based in Slovenia. We help organizations unlock business value through robust, value-focused solutions and thoughtful technical execution.

We specialize in rapid prototyping and building MVPs that evolve into production-ready systems, with a strong focus on early-stage innovation. Our team has collaborated with clients around the world, delivering projects that bridge cutting-edge AI research and real business impact.

## Key Focus Areas

- Rapid prototyping and MVP development
- Production-ready AI systems
- Early-stage innovation
- Value-focused technical solutions
- Bridging AI research and business impact
